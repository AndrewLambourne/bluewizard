Hogan's Foodery was founded in 1983 by Todd 'Zarathustra' Hogan. After a harrowing encounter with a slice of partially-chewed bologna, Todd claimed he had recieved an epiphany and promptly opened his own Americana-themed restaurant, the Foodery! For 36 years, Todd has been writing a personalized prophecy for each and every meal, a fun twist on the fortune cookie! So if you bite into one of our delicious burgers or juicy hot dogs and find a prognostication inside, don't worry- at the Foodery, it's fate!

Hogan's Foodery is located right in the bustling heart of downtown Centralia, Pennsylvania. Come on down and visit- we know we'll be seeing you soon!
